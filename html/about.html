Our Story

Started in Eugene Oregon in 2018, Alpha Sky has taken a few forms over the years. Ultimately settling in Portland as a nano home-brewery, pumping out 1/3 barrel batches. We may one day have flagships, but for now every batch is a new recipe. Quantities are limited, so contact us fast if you need supplies. Our motto – Brewing Beer That Tastes Like Beer means you wont find any pastry stouts, smoothie sours, or milkshake IPA’s.
